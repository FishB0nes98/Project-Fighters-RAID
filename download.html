Frequently Asked Questions

Is Project Fighters free to play?

Yes! Project Fighters is completely free to play. All characters, story campaigns, and game modes are accessible without any purchases required.

Do I need the launcher to play?

No, you can play directly in your browser! The launcher offers benefits like automatic updates and offline mode, but the web version has full functionality.

Can I play offline?

With the launcher, yes! Story mode and practice battles are available offline. However, online features like PvP and cloud save sync require an internet connection.

How often is the game updated?

We release regular updates with new characters, balance changes, and content. Major updates typically arrive monthly, with hotfixes as needed.

Is my progress saved in the cloud?

Yes! When logged in, your progress is automatically saved to the cloud and syncs across all devices, including browser and launcher versions.
